# Premier League Dashboard

Interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing the 2025-2026 Premier League 
season performance, standings, and relegation battle.

## Live Dashboard
[View on Tableau Public](https://public.tableau.com/views/PremierLeague_17743087690140/Dashboard1)

## Dataset
- Source: [FBref — Premier League Stats](https://fbref.com/en/comps/9/Premier-League-Stats)
- Fields: Teams, Goals For, Goals Against, Goals Diff, Wins, 
  Draws, Losses, Points, Attendance, Rank, Teams Groups

## Visualizations
- **Scatter Plot: Goals Difference** — Goals scored vs allowed, 
  colored by goal differential
- **Scatter Plot: Overall Performance** — Teams grouped by 
  Europe qualification, mid-table, and relegation zone
- **Text Table: Standings** — Full league table with Goals 
  Diff and final rank
- **Bar Chart: Goal Difference & Rankings** — Teams ranked 
  by goal difference, colored by group
- **Scatter Plot: Success vs Support** — Wins vs Attendance 
  with trend line showing fan support impact
- **Bar Chart: Relegation Battle** — Points per team with 
  relegation threshold reference line

## Key Insights
- Arsenal led the league with +39 goal difference
- Strong correlation between attendance and wins
- Clear separation between European spots and relegation zone
- Wolves and Burnley fought the closest relegation battle
